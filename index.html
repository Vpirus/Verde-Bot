Plant Care Basics

Watering

Water when the top inch of soil feels dry. Avoid overwatering.

Sunlight

Most indoor plants prefer bright, indirect light.

Feeding

Fertilize monthly during growing season (spring and summer).

Maintenance

Remove dead leaves and check for pests regularly.
