We're here to help your plants thrive!

Q: How do I know if I'm overwatering or underwatering?
A: Overwatering: Yellow leaves, mushy stems, moldy soil
Underwatering: Brown crispy edges, drooping, bone-dry soil
When in doubt, check the soil moisture with your finger before watering.

Q: Can you repot my plant for me?
A: Yes! Bring your plant to the nursery and we can repot it for a small service fee. We'll also show you how to do it yourself next time.
Q: What's the best plant for low light?
A: Snake Plant is your best bet! It can survive in very low light conditions. Golden Pothos is also quite tolerant of lower light.
Q: What's the easiest plant for absolute beginners?
A: We always recommend Snake Plant or Golden Pothos. Both are extremely forgiving and bounce back from mistakes easily.
Q: Which plants are safe for pets?
A: Boston Fern and Hibiscus are non-toxic to cats and dogs. Always supervise pets around plants, as even non-toxic plants can cause stomach upset if eaten in large quantities.
